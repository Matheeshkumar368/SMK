📐All Formulas
Core Rule — One Day Work
One Day Work = 1 / N
If someone finishes work in N days, they do 1/N of total work per day.
🔑 A finishes in 20 days → One day work = 1/20
Formula 1 — Together (Fractions)
1/A + 1/B = 1/Together
Add individual daily work fractions to get combined rate.
🔑 A=20d, B=30d → 1/20+1/30 = 5/60 = 1/12 → 12 days
Formula 2 — LCM Method (FASTER)
Total Work = LCM(days)
Assign LCM as total work. Each person does LCM÷days units per day. Add to get combined, then divide total by combined.
💡 A=20, B=30 → LCM=60 → A=3, B=2 → Together=5 → 60/5=12 days
Formula 3 — Men & Days
M₁ × D₁ = M₂ × D₂
Total man-days is constant. More men → fewer days. Inverse proportion.
🔑 20men × 10days = x × 5days → x = 40 men
Formula 4 — Extended MDH
M₁×D₁×H₁ = M₂×D₂×H₂
When hours per day also changes — includes hours worked per day.
🔑 Used when "working 8 hrs vs 10 hrs per day" type questions
Formula 5 — Efficiency
Efficiency ∝ 1/Time
More efficient = less time. If A takes 10 days and B takes 20, A is twice as efficient.
🔑 If A is 3x more efficient than B, A takes 1/3 the time
Formula 6 — Pipes
Filling: +1/n | Emptying: −1/n
Inlet pipe adds work. Outlet pipe subtracts work. Combine algebraically.
🔑 Fill in 20, Empty in 30 → Net = 1/20 − 1/30 = 1/60 → 60 min

⚡Shortcuts & Rules
🔥 LCM Method — Use This Always for Speed
1. Find LCM of all days given = Total work
2. Divide LCM by each person's days = units per day
3. Add units per day for "together"
4. Total Work ÷ Combined units = Days together
🔥 Efficiency Shortcut
A is 3 times as efficient as B → A takes 1/3 the time of B
B takes 30 days → A takes 10 days
If A is 20% more efficient → A takes 100/120 = 5/6 of B's time
❌ Never Add Days Directly!
WRONG: A takes 20 days, B takes 30 days → Together = 20+30 = 50 days ❌
RIGHT: Use 1/A + 1/B formula or LCM method → 12 days ✅

🧠Interactive Q&A
QUESTION 1 — EASY
A can complete work in 15 days and B in 10 days. How many days to complete together?
✅ B) 6 days

LCM(15,10) = 30 (Total Work)
A = 30/15 = 2 units/day | B = 30/10 = 3 units/day
Together = 5 units/day
Days = 30/5 = 6 days
QUESTION 2 — MODERATE
20 workers can complete a project in 12 days. How many workers needed to finish in 8 days?
✅ A) 30

M₁×D₁ = M₂×D₂
20 × 12 = x × 8
x = 240/8 = 30 workers
QUESTION 3 — EXAM MODERATE
A and B together can do work in 12 days. A alone takes 20 days. How long does B alone take?
✅ B) 30 days

1/B = 1/Together − 1/A = 1/12 − 1/20
= 5/60 − 3/60 = 2/60 = 1/30
B alone = 30 days

📐All Formulas
The Core Triangle
D = S × T
Distance = Speed × Time | S = D/T | T = D/S. The entire topic stems from this.
🔑 Cover distance → S×T | Find speed → D÷T | Find time → D÷S
Unit Conversion
km/hr × 5/18 = m/sec
km/hr to m/sec: multiply by 5/18 | m/sec to km/hr: multiply by 18/5
🔑 72 km/hr = 72×5/18 = 20 m/sec
Same Direction — Relative Speed
Relative = Bigger − Smaller
When two objects move in same direction, their relative speed is the difference.
🔑 80 km/hr and 60 km/hr same dir → Relative = 20 km/hr
Opposite Direction — Relative Speed
Relative = S1 + S2
When objects move toward each other or in opposite directions, add speeds.
🔑 80 + 60 = 140 km/hr relative speed
Average Speed
Avg = 2ab / (a+b)
When same distance is covered at two different speeds. Never simply average!
⚠️ Go 60, return 40 → Avg = 2×60×40/100 = 48 NOT 50
Boats — Downstream
D/S = Boat Speed + Stream
Going with current (downstream) adds stream speed to boat speed.
🔑 Boat=20, Stream=5 → Downstream=25 km/hr
Boats — Upstream
U/S = Boat Speed − Stream
Going against current (upstream) subtracts stream speed from boat speed.
🔑 Boat=20, Stream=5 → Upstream=15 km/hr
Train Crossing
Time = Length / Speed
Crossing pole: only train length. Crossing platform: train length + platform length. Always convert km/hr to m/sec!
⚠️ 200m train, 72km/hr → 200/20 = 10 sec

🧠Interactive Q&A
QUESTION 1 — EASY
A train 200 meters long travels at 72 km/hr. How long does it take to cross a pole?
✅ A) 10 seconds

Convert: 72 km/hr = 72 × 5/18 = 20 m/sec
Time = Length ÷ Speed = 200 ÷ 20 = 10 seconds
QUESTION 2 — MODERATE
A person goes from A to B at 60 km/hr and returns at 40 km/hr. What is the average speed?
✅ B) 48 km/hr

Average Speed = 2ab/(a+b) = 2×60×40/(60+40)
= 4800/100 = 48 km/hr
⚠️ Never (60+40)/2 = 50 — that's WRONG!
QUESTION 3 — EXAM MODERATE
Two trains running in opposite directions at 60 and 40 km/hr cross each other in 9 seconds. Their combined length is?
✅ C) 250m

Opposite direction → Relative = 60+40 = 100 km/hr
Convert: 100 × 5/18 = 250/9 m/sec
Combined length = Speed × Time = (250/9) × 9 = 250m
